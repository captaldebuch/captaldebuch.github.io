Development Log

June 2, 2026
Verdi Studio: A Philological Workstation for Symbolic Music

Initiated the core development of Verdi Studio, a specialized tool within the MELODY-SUITE designed for the philological study of Giuseppe Verdi's output. The application focuses on three primary pillars: (1) PDF-to-Symbolic conversion to unlock archival materials; (2) a Manual Annotation Layer for musicological grounding; and (3) a Verdi-specific RAG for intelligent score retrieval. This work is supported by Anatole Huel's summer internship, dedicated to building and curating the foundational Verdi Symbolic Dataset.

In Development Verdi Studio Internship
June 1, 2026
The MELODY-SUITE Ecosystem

Formalized the MELODY-SUITE architecture: a modular workstation for symbolic music. Integration of melody-crawl (automated discovery), melody-forge (instruction-tuning factory), and melody-local (unified inference gateway). This ecosystem enables a seamless transition from raw library discovery to high-fidelity AI generation.

Architecture Melody-Suite
Saturday, May 30, 2026
NotaGen Harmonic Fine-tuning & SCG Implementation

Launched Phase 1.3 of NotaGen training on Eurasia with %%COND tokens embedded across a 242-entry annotated corpus. Simultaneously implemented the Soft Constraint Guidance (SCG) gradient oracle loop for rule-guided diffusion.

hybrid-model Phase 2.1
Friday, May 29, 2026
VL-Constrained Diffusion Breakthrough

Training reached Epoch 20 on Eurasia. Results show a 99.8% reduction in parallel-fifth violations, unlocking the Phase 1 conditioned generation pipeline. Calibrated the Baroque Linter with a median score of 67.1 for authentic 4-voice scores.

Breakthrough Eurasia
Thursday, May 28, 2026
VAE-Diff Re-training Completed

Successfully re-trained VAE (30 epochs) and Diffusion (100 epochs) on corrected tokens. Fixed a critical tokenization bug and KL loss stability issues identified in the previous audit.
